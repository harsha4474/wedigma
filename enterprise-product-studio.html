Enterprise Product Studio

We Don't Build Projects.
We Build Products.

Most agencies think in projects—fixed timelines, handoffs, and moving on. We think in products—iterative development, ownership, and long-term impact. That's the difference between building software and building companies.

Philosophy

Product Mindset vs Project Mindset

Outcome Over Output

Projects measure success by delivered features. Products measure success by business impact—revenue, retention, conversion. We optimize for outcomes that move your metrics, not just shipping code.

Iteration Over Handoff

Projects end with a handoff document and goodbye. Products evolve through continuous iteration based on user data. We stay involved through launch, growth, and optimization phases.

Ownership Over Execution

Projects follow specs blindly. Products challenge assumptions, propose alternatives, and take ownership of success. We push back when specs don't align with user needs or business goals.

Learning Over Perfection

Projects aim for perfect v1.0 launches. Products ship MVPs to learn fast and iterate. We prioritize validated learning over theoretical perfection—ship, measure, improve.

Approach

How We Build Products

Product Discovery First

Every engagement starts with product discovery—not feature lists.

  • User research and interview analysis
  • Competitive landscape mapping
  • Business model validation
  • Core metric definition (North Star)

Build-Measure-Learn

We ship fast, measure everything, and iterate based on real user data.

  • MVP in 4-8 weeks (not 6 months)
  • Instrumented analytics from day one
  • Weekly metric reviews and sprint adjustments
  • A/B testing built into product DNA

Technical Excellence as Standard

Product thinking without technical depth is just PowerPoint. We bring both.

  • Senior engineers (8+ years avg experience)
  • Modern tech stack (React, Next.js, Node, AWS)
  • CI/CD, testing, monitoring from sprint 1
  • Architecture designed for scale, not rework

Journey

Your Product Journey with Wedigma

Week 1-2

Discovery & Strategy

Product discovery workshop, user research synthesis, technical architecture planning, and roadmap definition. We align on metrics that matter and build a shared understanding of product vision.

Week 3-6

Design & Prototyping

User flows, wireframes, high-fidelity designs, and interactive prototypes. User testing to validate assumptions before writing production code. Design system foundation for scalability.

Week 7-14

MVP Development

Core product build in 2-week sprints. Weekly demos and sprint reviews. Analytics instrumentation from day one. Infrastructure, CI/CD, and monitoring set up for production-ready launch.

Week 15-16

Launch & Validation

Beta launch with early users. Monitoring, bug fixes, and rapid iteration based on real usage data. Performance optimization and infrastructure scaling as needed.

Week 17+

Growth & Optimization

Feature iteration based on user feedback and analytics. Growth experiments, conversion optimization, and performance marketing. Long-term product partnership or team transition for internal ownership.
