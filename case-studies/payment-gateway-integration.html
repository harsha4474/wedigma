The Challenge

Complex Payment Compliance Requirements

PCI DSS Compliance

Required PCI DSS Level 1 certification to process credit cards. Needed secure tokenization, encrypted storage, and comprehensive audit logging without storing sensitive card data.

Fraud Detection

High-risk industry with significant fraud attempts. Needed real-time fraud scoring, velocity checks, and automated decision rules without adding friction to legitimate transactions.

Multi-Currency Support

International customer base requiring 30+ currencies, dynamic FX rates, and compliance with local payment regulations across multiple jurisdictions.

Reconciliation Complexity

Manual reconciliation between payment processor, bank accounts, and internal ledger taking 20+ hours monthly. Errors causing revenue leakage and accounting headaches.

Our Solution

Secure Payment Architecture

Stripe Integration Layer

PCI-compliant tokenization with Stripe Elements for secure card capture and processing.

  • Stripe Elements for PCI-compliant card input
  • Payment Intent API for 3D Secure (SCA)
  • Tokenization layer (no card storage)
  • Webhook-driven state management

Fraud Prevention Engine

Multi-layered fraud detection combining Stripe Radar with custom rules engine.

  • Stripe Radar ML fraud detection
  • Custom velocity checks (per card, per IP)
  • Geographic risk scoring
  • Automated decline rules (98% fraud block rate)

Automated Reconciliation

Daily automated reconciliation between Stripe payouts and internal ledger.

  • Automated payout matching
  • Multi-currency settlement tracking
  • Dispute and refund handling
  • Real-time Slack alerts for mismatches
